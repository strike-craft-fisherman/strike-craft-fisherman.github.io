Essential Workbench Tools

  • Carving Knife / Coping Saw: For profiling wood blanks (Balsa, Cedar, or Pine).
  • Sandpaper (120 to 400 grit): Crucial for smooth hydrodynamic finishes.
  • Hand Drill & Wire Twister: To install internal brass or stainless hook hangers.
  • Airbrush or Fine Brushes: For applying custom scale patterns and flash colors.

⚠️ Workshop Safety First

  • Respiratory Protection: Always wear a dust mask when sanding cedar or balsa—fine wood dust is hazardous.
  • Ventilation: Apply epoxy topcoats and solvent paints only in well-ventilated areas.
  • Blade Safety: Always carve away from your body; sharp tools require focus and leather carving gloves are highly recommended.
