Best lead weight distribution for stable 110mm glidebaits?

Started by LureCrafter99 • 2 hours ago
2 replies
LureCrafter99 (Topic Starter):
Hey guys, I'm carving a 110mm wooden glidebait but it keeps rolling on faster retrieves. Where should I center the belly weights?
BaitMaster_Tx:
Place 65% of the total ballast weight right in front of the belly hanger hook, and the remaining 35% slightly behind it. Keep it as low as possible near the keel line to stop the roll.
SaltyAngler:
Agreed with BaitMaster. If it still rolls, your line tie eyelet might be slightly off-center. Check that too!
